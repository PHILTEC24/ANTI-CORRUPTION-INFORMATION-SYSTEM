What the system does

Built for both sides of a report

Every feature exists to move a case from "filed" to "resolved" without losing the paper trail.

01

Secure reporting

Submit a title, category, and detailed description in a guided form, with your account protected by Supabase Authentication.

02

Location pinning

Drop a pin on an interactive map to show exactly where an incident occurred, helping administrators spot hotspots.

03

Evidence upload

Attach images and documents directly to a report, stored securely in the cloud alongside the case file.

04

Anonymous submission

Choose to file without attaching your identity to the report, while still being able to track its progress.

05

Status tracking

Follow a report through Pending, Under Review, Resolved, or Rejected, so you always know where it stands.

06

Admin oversight

Administrators review every case, update its status, manage user roles, and export summary reports as PDF.

About ACIS

A paper trail that's hard to bury

Corruption thrives where reports go missing or nobody follows up. ACIS keeps every case in one system, visible to the person who filed it and the administrator handling it, from submission to resolution.

The goal isn't just collecting reports. It's making sure each one gets a documented outcome.

Register and log in

Submit a report

Track the outcome

Watch the status update in your dashboard as the case moves toward resolution.
